⚠️ Important Note about QR Codes

QR codes are complex and may not work well as AR markers. For best results:

  • Use simple, high-contrast images or logos
  • Ensure the pattern has distinctive features
  • QR codes will be simplified to 16x16 patterns, losing their original functionality

How to Use:

1. Upload an image (simple patterns work better than QR codes)

2. Adjust the threshold to get clear black and white contrast

3. Set border size (larger borders improve tracking)

4. Enable "Crop center area" for QR codes to focus on the main pattern

5. Generate and download both the .patt file and marker image


AR.js Usage:

• Use the .patt file in your AR.js code

• Print the marker image for tracking

• Ensure good lighting and stable camera for best results
